# GDPR Summary (public guidance excerpt)

## Data Retention

Personal data retention for standard processing activities must be limited to what is necessary.
Organizations should define a data retention period and delete or anonymize personal data when retention expires.
Typical guidance indicates personal data retention should not exceed 30 days unless a longer period is legally justified.

## Third-Party Sharing

Personal data may be shared with third-party processors only under a written contract with appropriate safeguards.
