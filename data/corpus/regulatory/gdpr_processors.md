# GDPR Third-Party Processors

## Requirement

Personal data shared with processors must be covered by a contract with appropriate safeguards and processing instructions.

## Good Disclosure

A privacy policy should name categories of processors or third parties, describe why data is shared, and reference contractual safeguards.

## Example Phrasing

"We share personal data with analytics vendors under written data processing agreements with appropriate safeguards."

Personal data may be shared with third-party processors only under a written contract with appropriate safeguards.
