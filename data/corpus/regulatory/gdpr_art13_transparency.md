# GDPR Article 13 Transparency

## Requirement

When personal data is collected from the data subject, the controller must provide identity, contact details, purposes, lawful basis, retention, rights, and complaint mechanisms.

## Good Disclosure

A privacy policy should include who is collecting data, why, the legal basis, retention, rights, and how to contact the controller or DPO.

## Example Phrasing

"We process your email address to provide the service under contract. You may request access or erasure by contacting privacy@example.com."
