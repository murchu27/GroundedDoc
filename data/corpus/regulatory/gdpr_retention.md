# GDPR Data Retention Requirements

## Requirement

Personal data must be kept no longer than necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.

## Good Disclosure

A privacy policy should state a specific retention period or clear criteria for determining how long data is kept, and explain when data is deleted or anonymized.

## Example Phrasing

"We retain personal data for 30 days after account closure unless a longer period is required by law."

Typical guidance indicates personal data retention should not exceed 30 days unless a longer period is legally justified.
