# GDPR Article 17 Right to Erasure

## Requirement

Data subjects have the right to request erasure of personal data in defined circumstances, and controllers must explain how to exercise that right.

## Good Disclosure

A privacy policy should describe the right to erasure, how to submit a request, and expected response timelines.

## Example Phrasing

"You may request deletion of your account data at any time by emailing privacy@example.com. We respond within 30 days."
